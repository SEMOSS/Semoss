Reference Page:
  • Capability Description: This information is directly stated within the respective CONOPS document
  • Capability Group: This information is directly stated within the respective CONOPS document
  • Mission Outcomes: This information is directly stated within the respective CONOPS document
  • Participants: The participants for the capabilities are mapped through the associated tasks using the HL7 Healthcare Scenario Roadmap as a reference
  • CONOPS Source: Identifies whether this capability is defined within the Health Services Delivery (HSD), Health Systems Support (HSS) or Force Health Protection (FHP) CONOPS
  • Tasks: This information is directly stated within the respective CONOPS document
  • Business Processes: This information is mapped from the capability to the respective supporting business processes that are comprised of clinical, logistical and business bps.
  • Business Rule: This information is directly stated within the respective CONOPS document
  • Business Standards: This information is directly stated within the respective CONOPS document
  • Technical Requirements and Standards: This information is derived from mapping the capability attributes within the CONOPS to the Health Standards Profile (HSP)
  • Business Logic Units: These are the algorithms that are needed to complete the tasks that support the capability and were mapped by subject matter experts
  • Functional Gaps: This depicts the functional gaps that are affecting the capability
  • Data Objects (Provide and Consume): These are the data objects that are needed to complete the tasks that support the capability and were mapped by subject matter experts
